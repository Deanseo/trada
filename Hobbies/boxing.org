I started boxing as a hobby a couple years ago.

Since that I've realized it offers numerous beneifts for self-discipline, health, and my stress management.
It's tough; requiring a high level of dedication and consistency, through which it instills discipline in all areas of life.

Apart from that, I think boxing is also an excellent way to stay physically healthy.
This sport provides a full-body workout which incrases strength, agility, and endurance.

Most importantly, it's really a powerful tool for stress management.
I used to run at night, whenever I was stressd. I feel like I discovered a better method though.

It definitely triggers the release of endorphins at a greater scale, lifting my mood and helping me get ready for the next day's work.
Through this hobby ones can find a balance between physical exertion and mental relaxation for a more holistic life style.
